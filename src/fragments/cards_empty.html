Blank Cards

The wilds are a dangerous, unknowable place—you can never fully predict what will happen on any given adventure. This supplement contains a variety of premade card decks for you to use in your games—but if you need a set of custom cards for your adventures then look no further.

Combine these empty card fronts with your choice of card back to create a custom deck for your own games.

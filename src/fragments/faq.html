FAQ

This section lists at some of the frequently asked questions regarding the Card Codex. If your question isn't answered here, feel free to contact me.

AWho's the artist?

The card artwork (including the other artwork in this book) was created by me—Giffyglyph. The base heading icons were created by the artists of game-icons.net.

  • Card Artwork: Giffyglyph
  • Header Icons: www.game-icons.net

CI have a card/deck suggestion

If you have a suggestion about new cards or card decks—or you've noticed an error in an existing card—contact me to let me know.

DCan I reference this?

Feel free to reference parts of this book in your own free homebrew—with an appropriate credit back to me. But please don't replicate my work wholesale, nor use it in any for-sale variant or product.

ECan I sell decks with this art?

No—the artwork in this supplement is provided for personal use only. You may print card decks for your own table and games, but please don't share, upload, or resell the art for any other purpose.

FCan I buy a printed deck?

Not at the moment, no. I'm currently looking into this as a few people have requested printed decks. In the meantime, this project (and most of my other work) is released on Pay-What-You-Want terms.
